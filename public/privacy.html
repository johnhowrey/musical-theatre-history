Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Overture does not collect, store, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no advertising, and no third-party analytics or tracking — in the app or on this website.

Information we don’t collect

We don’t ask for your name, email address, location, contacts, or any other personal data. Overture has no sign-in and no user profiles.

Information stored on your device

The app remembers your last map view (zoom level and position) so it reopens where you left off. This is saved only on your own device and is never transmitted to us.

Map data

To display the map, the app downloads a public data file describing the network of shows and creators. This request contains no personal information about you.

Server logs

Like most websites, our hosting provider may automatically record standard technical request information (such as IP address and browser type) in server logs for security and reliability. We do not use this information to identify you, and we do not combine it with any other data.

Children

Overture collects no data from anyone, including children, and is suitable for all ages.

Third parties

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the “Last updated” date above will reflect the latest version.
